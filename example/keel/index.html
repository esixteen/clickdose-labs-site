The Honest Filter

Who should choose which

Keel is built for you if

  • You're past ~$250K revenue, or carrying inventory, or invoicing on terms
  • You've outgrown "I'll ask support and hear back Thursday"
  • You want your CPA and your bookkeeper talking to each other in January
  • You'd rather pay one honest flat price than watch tiers creep

Choose Brightbooks if

  • You're cash-basis, low-volume, and mostly need clean records for filing
  • Budget is the deciding factor and $199 vs $349 settles it
  • You rarely need to talk to a human about your numbers

Fair Questions

Asked on almost every fit call

Is switching mid-year going to wreck my books?

No. The parallel month exists exactly so nothing breaks. We rebuild your last three months first, so your year is continuous in one file, and your CPA gets a cleaner January than they've ever had from you.

Am I locked into a contract?

Month to month, cancel by email, and your books leave with you the same day because they were always in your QuickBooks or Xero account. We keep clients by being good, not by being sticky.

Do you replace my CPA?

No, we make them faster. We do the monthly books; your CPA does tax strategy and filing with a clean, reconciled file and direct access to your bookkeeper in January.

What if I'm happy with Brightbooks?

Then stay. Honestly. If your books are cash-basis and simple and their price works, switching buys you little. Bookmark us for the month your volume tier jumps or your first inventory order lands.
