The wakeup isn't random. It's chemistry.

You fall asleep fine. Then at 3:07am your eyes open like someone flipped a switch, and your brain hands you a highlight reel of everything you have ever said in a meeting.

Melatonin does not fix this. Melatonin is a timing signal that tells your body when night starts. It was never designed to keep you asleep at 3am, which is why you wake up groggy AND you woke up anyway.

Before You Ask

Fair questions about a big claim

Will it knock me out like a sleeping pill?

No, and that is deliberate. Sable lowers the volume instead of cutting the power. You drift off because your body is finally quiet, which is why you can still get up if the baby cries, and why there is no groggy tax in the morning.

Is it habit-forming?

The ingredients are non-habit-forming. The warm-cup ritual, we admit, is habit-forming in the way brushing your teeth is. That is a feature.

Can I take it with melatonin or my current routine?

Most people use Sable to replace melatonin, not stack on it. If you take prescription sleep medication or any regular medication, talk to your doctor before adding anything, including this.

What if it does nothing for me?

Then it costs you nothing. Thirty nights, every dollar back, refund out within 24 hours of your email, and you keep the canister. Sleep is too personal for us to argue with your experience.

Why 30 nights and not "tonight"?

You will likely feel the first shift by night three. But sleep is a pattern, not an event, and patterns take weeks to rebuild. One canister is 30 servings on purpose: it is exactly one honest trial.
