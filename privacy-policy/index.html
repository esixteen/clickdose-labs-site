Privacy Policy

Last updated: June 8, 2026

This Privacy Policy explains how ClickDose Labs ("ClickDose Labs," "we," "us," or "our") collects, uses, discloses, and safeguards information when you visit clickdoselabs.com, contact us, or engage our services. We keep this short and plain. If anything is unclear, email us.

1. Who we are

ClickDose Labs is a performance marketing and customer acquisition firm based in Ontario, Canada. For privacy questions, you can reach us at info@clickdose.com.

2. Information we collect

  • Information you give us. Your name, email address, company, phone number, and anything else you share when you book a call, fill out a form, or correspond with us.
  • Information collected automatically. Standard log and analytics data such as IP address, browser type, device, pages viewed, referring URLs, and timestamps, collected through cookies and similar technologies.
  • Client engagement data. When you become a client, we process information needed to deliver our services, which may include access to advertising accounts, CRM data, and campaign performance data, governed by our separate service agreement.

3. How we use information

  • To respond to inquiries and schedule and conduct strategy calls.
  • To provide, operate, and improve our services.
  • To send relevant communications about our services, which you can opt out of at any time.
  • To analyze site usage and improve performance and content.
  • To comply with legal obligations and protect our rights.

4. Cookies and analytics

We use cookies and third-party analytics tools to understand how visitors use the site. You can control cookies through your browser settings. Disabling cookies may affect some site functionality.

5. Third parties we work with

We share information only as needed to operate our business, including with service providers such as scheduling tools (e.g. Cal.com), analytics providers, email and hosting platforms, and advertising platforms (e.g. Meta, Google, LinkedIn). These providers process information on our behalf under their own terms. We do not sell your personal information.

6. Data retention

We keep personal information only as long as needed for the purposes described here, to comply with legal and contractual obligations, and to resolve disputes. When it is no longer needed, we delete or anonymize it.

7. Your rights

Depending on where you live, you may have the right to access, correct, delete, or restrict the use of your personal information, and to withdraw consent. Canadian residents have rights under PIPEDA, and residents of other regions (such as the EU/UK under GDPR) may have additional rights. To exercise any of these, email info@clickdose.com and we will respond within a reasonable timeframe.

8. Security

We use reasonable administrative, technical, and physical safeguards to protect information. No method of transmission or storage is completely secure, so we cannot guarantee absolute security.

9. International transfers

We operate from Canada and work with providers who may store or process data in other countries. Where required, we take steps to ensure appropriate protection for cross-border transfers.

10. Children

Our site and services are intended for businesses and are not directed to children under 16. We do not knowingly collect information from children.

11. Changes to this policy

We may update this policy from time to time. The "Last updated" date above reflects the most recent version. Material changes will be posted on this page.
