The System

Why our emails land in the inbox, not the spam folder.

Most cold email fails on one thing: deliverability. You can have a perfect list and perfect copy, but if you land in spam, none of it matters. Here's how we engineer inbox placement.

AI list-building in Clay

Waterfall enrichment pulls the right contacts from multiple data sources, and every email is verified and bounce-checked before a single message goes out.

Dedicated domains & inboxes

We send from separate domains built for outbound, never your primary domain. Your main company email reputation stays protected no matter what.

Real inbox warmup

Smartlead warms every inbox over weeks and keeps warming in the background, building the sender reputation that gets you into the inbox and keeps you there.

Throttled, rotated sending

Human-like send volumes, spread across inboxes and ramped gradually. Campaigns scale without tripping spam filters or torching deliverability.
